Pseudocode

START WHILE (NOT AT EXIT)
IF (RIGHT SIDE IS CLEAR AND NOT VISITED)
TURN RIGHT 90 degrees
GO FORWARDS 10 cm
ELSE IF (FRONT SIDE IS CLEAR AND NOT VISITED)
GO FORWARDS 10 cm
ELSE IF (LEFT SIDE IS CLEAR AND NOT VISITED)
TURN LEFT 90 degrees
GO FORWARDS 10 cm
ELSE
TURN AROUND 180 degrees
GO FORWARDS 10 cm
END IF
END WHILE
STOP
